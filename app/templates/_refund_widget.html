About the data we collect

We don't want your info - we just want to measure and analyze data with respect to Go Transit delays. We specifically collect: date travelled, departing/arriving stations and departure time. That's it.

With this crowd-sourced data, we can help alert you and others to what trips refunds are being submitted for - so that you can claim yours too!
